Looking Back, Moving Forward: Our Journey from 7-Sampaguita to 8-Rizal

July 2026

Back then, we were 7-Sampaguita. We made so many memories that time can't bring back. Noong Grade 7, aminadong maiingay at palaging magulo kami—kahit may guro sa harapan, nag-iingay pa rin kami! But that didn't stop us from excelling. Nanatili pa rin kaming masipag, mahusay, at aktibo sa mga activities. Tuwing napapagalitan kami ng aming guro dahil sa kakulitan, marunong kaming humingi ng paumanhin at mag-sorry.

Isang hindi malilimutang alaala noong nag-birthday ang aming adviser—sobrang saya namin noon dahil sinurpresa namin si Ma'am! Maaga kaming pumasok lahat para maghanda, and it was just pure happiness from that day on.

Now that we are in Grade 8, some of our old classmates have moved on, and new students have joined our family. Thanks to our adviser, we all got to know each other after just a week! As we begin this new chapter as Grade 8-Rizal, buong-puso naming susundin ang sinabi ng ating pambansang bayani na si Dr. Jose P. Rizal: "Ang kabataan ang pag-asa ng bayan." Kaya naman, sama-sama nating tahakin ang mga problema, bigyan ito ng solusyon, at sabay-sabay nating harapin ang mga hamon patungo sa susunod na henerasyon!
